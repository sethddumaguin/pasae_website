Our Mission

Established in 1988, the Pilipino Association of Scientists, Architects, and Engineers (PASAE) serves as a close-knit support group for students in the technical fields, providing student members a space for social interaction, cultural understanding, professional guidance, and academic support.


PASAE

 

What We Do

PASAE provides a variety of events and resources for our members. We provide a social and cultural space for students in the technical fields to cultivate connections with students of similar backgrounds and de-stress through events like PAS’Glade and Chill and PASAE’ce Skating. Professionally and academically, we provide workshops and access to our network of alumni working in industry through events like our Alumni Mixer and Gilead Panel.

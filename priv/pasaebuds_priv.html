What is PASAEBuds?


PASAEBuds is a bi-weekly activity for members to interact with another PASAE member. Hangout with your PASAEBud and send a picture of you two in our PASAE'dkicks Spring '19 chat for a chance to win a prize. (One picture per pair) Past winners have won a Starbuck's giftcard, Jamba Juice giftcard, and 3D printed nameplate. Check out some of our past semester's PASAEBuds!

Pathways | A Graduate Student Panel

Open to everyone!

Pathways is a graduate student panel meant to empower and inform Pilipinx undergraduate students about graduate school. This event is hosted by Pilipinx Academic Student Services (PASS), in collaboration with Partnership for Pre-Professional Pilipinxs (P4), Pilipinx Association for Health Careers (PAHC), Pilipinx American Alliance (PAA), and Pilipinx Association of Scientists, Architects, and Engineers (PASAE).

Come enjoy a nice dinner while listening to panelists discuss their motivation for attending grad school and their experiences. Everyone is welcome! Whether you are set on grad school or are still exploring your options, this event is for you! You do not need to be Pilipinx, and recent Cal grads are welcome as well! It will be an opportunity for current students to hear from and network with esteemed professionals who will share insight on their work industries, and provide any advice they think is helpful as UC Berkeley students move toward graduate school and their careers.

Attire is smart casual, but dress to impress!

  • Date: Mar. 3
  • Where: Alumni House
  • Non-Alumni RSVP: Here
  • Alumni RSVP: Here

Spring Infosession

Open to everyone!

Established in 1988, the Pilipino Association of Scientists, Architects, and Engineers (PASAE) serves as a close-knit support group for students in the technical fields, providing student members a space for social interaction, cultural understanding, professional guidance, and academic support.

Come discover the many amazing and enjoyable opportunities that PASAE has to offer! Meet our core members, other students interested in technical majors, as well as students in the Pil Community!

And of course, free food will be provided!

  • Where: 242 Dwinelle

FASTERCON

Open to everyone!

FASTERCON is the annual conference hosted at UC Berkeley by FASTER - an organization that aims to create and cultivate a valuable movement of Fil-Americans in Silicon Valley. Every year, the conference highlights and celebrates the work of leading Filipino American technology professionals making an impact on the tech industry. Last year's keynote speaker is serial entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and tech pioneer, and PhilDev founder, Dado Banatao.

  • Date: Oct. 6, 2018
  • Where: International House

    Donate


    Where does my donation go?

    Donations go towards funding PASAE events such as the Alumni Dinner, Wine & Cheese Mixer, and PASAE Banquet. Through donations, we are able to make these events free for all to attend. Donations go towards the venue, food, and other logistical costs. Thus, members and community members pay no cost to attend events such as our Alumni Dinner or Wine & Cheese Mixer.
